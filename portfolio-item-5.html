    TMC PULSE

    Project Description

    The Texas Medical Center is one of the best medical centers in the United States. People come from all over the world for treatment. The TMC Pulse is their magazine that highlights everything going on within the TMC. I was able to work on the magazine in many facets including directing photoshoots, layout, graphics and production.
